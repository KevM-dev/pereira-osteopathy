About the practice

Treating the cause, not just the ache

Pereira Osteopathy & Wellness is an independent practice built on one idea: your body is a connected system, and lasting relief comes from understanding how it all fits together — not from chasing symptoms one at a time.

Every appointment starts with a proper conversation and a full postural and movement assessment, so treatment is built around your history, your daily life, and how you actually move — whether that's at a desk, on a pitch, or carrying a toddler on one hip.

What I offer

Treatments & care

Four strands of hands-on care, drawn on individually or combined in one session depending on what your body needs.

Osteopathy

Whole-body manual assessment and treatment for back and neck pain, joint stiffness, sports strains and postural complaints — using gentle, precise techniques suited to you.

Massage

Deep tissue and sports massage to release tight muscles, improve circulation and speed up recovery between training blocks or long working weeks.

Manual Therapy

Targeted hands-on joint mobilisation and soft-tissue work for long-standing stiffness, old injuries, and areas that don't respond to rest alone.

Wellness

Postural guidance, tailored rehab exercises and lifestyle advice so the benefit of each session lasts well beyond the treatment table.
